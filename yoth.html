About YOTH

Long before we designed our first logo, or unboxed any merchandise orders, Yoth was something that far surpassed anything materialistic. It represented a group of nine high school friends with roots in Canton, Michigan. Each year, they would design a piece of streetwear inspired apparel named after their unique group chat. When people caught a glimpse of their designs, they questioned the meaning of Yoth, and quite frankly, there wasn’t a clear answer its members could give. Although this stumped them for years, they slowly established their own definition: The camaraderie of a friendship and all of the indescribable moments and experiences associated with it. It’s the late-night adventures and laughs, the tough conversations that spark self-improvement, and the tear-filled accounts that are almost therapeutic. Using brotherhood or sisterhood to describe it would be a disservice. It’s Yoth. So, whether you are ordering a fresh new hoodie, keeping up on our socials, or simply reading this message, we humbly extend an invitation to our family. However, when you wake up and represent the Yoth Brand, remember that this is a challenge to be the best version of yourself and to value people as the world’s greatest assets. After all, this product wouldn’t have been possible without the friendship it blossomed from.
